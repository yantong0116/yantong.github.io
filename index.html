Lung Nodule Classification

July 2022 - Currently

Construct the 3D lung with DICOM files from the LIDC-IDRI dataset. After extracting the lung nodule based on the center of the nodule with size 48 x 48 x 48, I get the axial, coronal, and sagittal slices of the nodule. Then I use pyradiomics GLCM, GLDM, and GLRLM methods to extract the texture of the nodules. After the image pre-processing is finished, I concatenate the axial, coronal and sagittal to a multi-view image. Then concatenate the texture multi-view images with the original multi-view images. Then augment the nodules to 16 times. Finally, I load the data to train on deep learning neural network to get the nodule benign or malignant classification performance.
